Effective Date: June 15, 2026

VOCAreps (“Service Provider”) operates the VOCAre mobile application (“App”). This Privacy Policy explains how personal information is collected, used, stored, and shared when users use the App.

1. Information We Collect

The Service Provider may collect or process the following information as necessary to provide the App.

  • Account information: name, email address, user ID, and login session information provided through Google Sign-In or Sign in with Apple
  • Learning data: vocabulary folders, word lists, words, meanings, examples, questions, answers, choices, explanations, memorization status, test results, wrong-answer notes, review records, study plans, and learning progress
  • Rewards and purchase-related information: attendance records, credits, test passes, word slots, ad reward records, in-app product IDs, and purchase verification records
  • App usage information: in-app activity, learning activity, error logs, and performance information
  • Device and identifier information: device type, operating system, app version, IP address, advertising identifier, and approximate location
  • Notification-related information: notification settings or notification tokens needed to provide review reminders or similar notification features

The Service Provider does not directly collect payment card numbers, bank account numbers, or other payment method details. In-app purchases are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play.

2. How We Use Information

  • Creating accounts, signing users in, identifying users, and maintaining sessions
  • Saving, backing up, syncing, and restoring learning data
  • Providing vocabulary learning, tests, wrong-answer notes, review features, and study plans
  • Managing attendance rewards, ad rewards, credits, passes, word slots, and in-app purchase benefits
  • Providing ads, measuring ad performance, and preventing abuse or fraud
  • Analyzing errors, improving performance, and maintaining security
  • Responding to user inquiries
  • Complying with legal obligations and handling disputes

3. Third-Party Services

The App may use the following third-party services.

  • Supabase: account, database, server storage, and synchronization
  • Google Sign-In: Google account login
  • Sign in with Apple: Apple account login
  • Google AdMob and Google Mobile Ads SDK: advertising, ad rewards, and ad performance measurement
  • Google Play: Android in-app purchase processing
  • Apple App Store: iOS in-app purchase processing

These third-party services may process information according to their own privacy policies.

  • Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob Partner Sites
  • Apple Privacy Policy

The Service Provider does not sell users’ personal information.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

The Service Provider retains personal information for as long as necessary to provide the App.

When a user deletes their account, the account and related learning data will be deleted or scheduled for deletion. However, minimal records necessary for security, abuse prevention, purchase verification, refund handling, legal compliance, or deletion verification may be retained for up to 90 days. If a longer retention period is required by applicable law, the information may be retained for the period required by law.

Users may request account deletion in the App.

  • App > Settings > Account Management > Delete Account

Users may also request deletion by email.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not primarily directed to children under the age of 16. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personal information from children where parental or guardian consent is required by applicable law.

If a parent or guardian becomes aware that a child has provided personal information without proper consent, they may contact the Service Provider to request deletion.
