한국어

VOCAreps provides support for the VOCAre mobile application (the "App"). If you need help with app use, accounts, payment checks, undelivered purchases, or privacy matters, contact us by email.

Support Email

vocare2605@gmail.com

1. What You Can Contact Us About

  • App errors or usability issues
  • Account sign-in, account linking, or account deletion questions
  • Vocabulary, tests, wrong-answer notes, review, or planner features
  • Purchases that were not delivered in the App
  • Help checking payment history
  • Privacy Policy or privacy rights requests

2. Helpful Information to Include

To help us review your request faster, please include the information below when relevant.

  • Inquiry type
  • Email address used to sign in to VOCAre
  • Device and operating system
  • The screen where the issue occurred and a short description
  • Order number or receipt information for purchase-related inquiries
  • A screenshot if an error screen is shown

3. Payments and Refunds

Refund eligibility and final handling for purchases made through the App Store or Google Play are governed by each store's policy and process. If you want to request a refund, use the official pages below.

  • Apple Refund Request
  • Google Play Refund Help

If a purchase was not delivered in the App or you need help checking an order, contact vocare2605@gmail.com. Including your order number or receipt information helps us review it faster.

4. Account Deletion and Privacy Requests

You can request account deletion in the App.

  • VOCAre App > Settings > Account Management > Delete Account

If you cannot use the App, you may also send account deletion or privacy requests by email.

  • vocare2605@gmail.com
